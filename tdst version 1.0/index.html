About us

We heard bedtime stories, narrated to us by our grandparents…… And then many more stories were being formed in our young little minds… To create a means through which we could reach out to the global entertainment industry, to narrate the stories we dreamt off, we were born out of an idea that led to a discussion with a brilliant outcome to create different stories under varied genres. Each story is our brainchild, where a story is born, nurtured and developed akin to a child.

Our grandparents were storytellers...

Philosophy

We all know that a storyteller is one who creates emotions and drama to enthral the listener with his hypnotic and engaging way of storytelling. Our grandparents were proof of that.Even an ordinary idea can create an extraordinary world, full of possibilities, innovation and originality. Based on the same philosophy, we amalgamate and weave our ideas to create an imaginary world of stories that give birth to some brilliant characters and emotions leading to beautiful and enjoyable experiences.We believe that storytelling is the best medium through which we can bring life to our words written on paper and make others visualise our imaginary world. THE DREAM STORYTELLER also believes in converting your dreams into reality by offering scripts that are nothing short of classic tales and fantasies.
